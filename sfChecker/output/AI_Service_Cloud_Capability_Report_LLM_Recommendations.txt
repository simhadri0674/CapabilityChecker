**Practical Improvement Steps:**

1. **Assess Business Requirements**: Identify key business objectives and prioritize missing capabilities accordingly.
2. **Develop a Roadmap**: Create a phased implementation plan to ensure a structured approach.
3. **Establish a Center of Excellence**: Designate a team to oversee the implementation and maintenance of new capabilities.
4. **Provide Training and Support**: Ensure that end-users are trained and supported throughout the implementation process.

**3-Phase Roadmap:**

**Phase 1: Immediate (0-3 months)**

1. Implement Escalation Rules and Auto Response Rules to improve case management.
2. Enable Email to Case and Web to Case to streamline case creation.
3. Introduce Knowledge Base and Quick Text to enhance agent productivity.
4. Develop a basic Service Console to provide a unified workspace for agents.

**Phase 2: Mid-term (3-6 months)**

1. Implement Omni Channel Routing and CTI Integration to enhance customer engagement.
2. Develop Entitlements and Milestones to manage service agreements and track time-bound steps.
3. Introduce Macros and Automation Flows to automate repetitive tasks.
4. Establish a basic Customer Community to provide self-service capabilities.

**Phase 3: Advanced (6-12 months)**

1. Implement Field Service Management and Einstein Bots to enhance field operations and customer interactions.
2. Develop Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and Service Analytics to measure performance and optimize operations.
3. Introduce Incident Management, Problem Management, and Change Management to improve service quality.
4. Establish a comprehensive Customer Feedback and Survey program to measure customer satisfaction.

By following this roadmap, the organization can systematically address missing capabilities, improve service operations, and enhance customer experiences.
